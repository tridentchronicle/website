Customized track and trace solution for supply chain

Our codes help you to track and trace the products at each and every point in the supply chain, whether its container, shipping container, distributor, retailer or end user (with location information of each scan point).

Track and trace solution for any product

  • We help you laser engrave or print the track code on each and every component parts (such as chassis, brake-pad, fuel pump etc) of your product item (such as automobile).
  • You can track and trace any component part used in the product item using the tracking code printed on the product item.
  • You can track and trace any product item or other component parts used in it, from the tracking code printed on any of the component parts.
  • You can track and trace any product item or component parts by vendor, date and assembly line and location used.
  • This can be integrated with the Supply Chain for further tracing and tracking of your product items among distributors and end users.
